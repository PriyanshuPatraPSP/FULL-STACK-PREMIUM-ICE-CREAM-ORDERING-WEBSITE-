The Beginning

L'Artisan Crème was born from a simple obsession: the perfect scoop. Founder Marie Dumont spent six years traveling through Italy, France, and Japan — learning the ancient art of gelato, glace, and kakigori before returning to Paris with a vision.

Her first small shop opened in Le Marais in 2012, and within a month, there was a queue down the block every morning. The secret? Radical simplicity — the finest ingredients, the slowest churning, and zero shortcuts.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great ice cream is a form of meditation. Every flavor we create goes through at least 40 iterations before it reaches you. We taste blindly, we adjust obsessively, and we never rush.

Our dairy comes from a 200-acre farm in Normandy. Our vanilla beans are hand-selected in Madagascar by the same family for 10 years. Our pistachios come from a single grove in Bronte, Sicily.
